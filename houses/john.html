More about Jonh:

The Cottage of 2990 blends traditional charm with futuristic technology. From the outside, it looks like a classic country home with its thatched roof and ivy-clad walls.
Inside, however, it's equipped with advanced automation. Windows and doors feature smart glass for privacy and protection, while retractable shudders guard against unwanted animals.
A sophisticated sound recognition system helps manage these features effortlessly.

Convenience is central to the cottage's design. Voice-activated systems control everything from scheduling and weather updates to meal preparation. The breakfast stove cooks personalized meals,
while the patio tables adjust for dining or entertainment. Automated bed warmers and bath systems ensure comfort, and garden sprinklers optimize watering with minimal effort.
Cleaning is handled by robotic systems, including mouse-like devices for dusting and copper scrap rats for trash removal.

Safety is meticulously managed with an advanced fire alarm system, automated water controls, and robotic units that handle leaks and chemical spills. The study area features a modern fireplace
and poetry reader for relaxation, while the nursery boasts interactive media and glass walls for a secure, stimulating environment. This integration of old-world aesthetics with high-tech conveniences
makes the Cottage of 2990 a unique and comfortable retreat.
